About Us

With over 30 years of mechanical experience, Bobby 'Porky' Underwood has worked on everything from daily drivers to heavy-duty trucks to farming equipment and more. Specializing in automotive and diesel repair, he founded Porky's Portable Pitstop to make car repairs more convenient for you.

Whether you're stuck on the side of the road or just need an oil change while you're at work, we bring the garage to you. Our mission is to provide reliable, efficient, and honest mobile mechanic services—because car trouble shouldn't mean rearranging your whole day.
